WHAT WE OFFER

TUM.ai has many offers for students of any university.
Some of these opportunities are open to the public, others are exclusively for our members. 

The following opportunities are open to the public:
Workshops, Events, Talks and Job Offers

The following opportunities are for members only:
Internal Projects, Social Events, Community

START YOUR JOURNEY AT TUM.ai

At TUM.ai, students come together to collaborate on technical projects and turn theory into practice.
Our projects focus on innovative technologies around the field of artificial intelligence.
As a group, we exchange knowledge, participate in meet-ups and practice our skills together.

Your Path At Tum.ai

Join TUM.ai

Submit your application on our website and tell us about your skills, interests and experience.
We will then do a short interview to see if you fit into the group.

TUM.ai Member

After your admission, you are now a part of our community.
You can now engage actively in our community life and participate in internal projects.
Every TUM.ai member also works in an organizational taskforce.

Internal Projects

Work on your own ideas to apply AI using our computational resources. You can also get help and mentoring on an entrepreneurial path.

Organisation

Every TUM.ai member is a part of the management team. TUM.ai is organized in 9 divisions, all containing multiple task forces.

Community

As a TUM.ai member, you can now participate in our social events, meetups and get to know like-minded people from the community.

TUM.AI JOBs

You want to gain practical experience by helping companies solve problems with artificial intelligence?
TUM.ai has many company partners which are looking for help at applying AI.
All job offers provided by our partnerts are posted publicly, so everybody can apply!

Team work

Many of TUM.ai's job opportunites require you to work in a team. Get to know new people and grow your connections!

REAL WORLD PROJECTS

The TUM.ai job offers are your opportunity to work on a real-life problem involving AI, not one of those toy problems used at university.

WIP

The TUM.ai Management Team is currently working on a detailled plan about job offers. More details on ways of distribution will be published soon.

TUM.AI LEARNING

You want to learn more about Artificial Intelligence?
TUM.ai and its partners offer workshops, talks and events to do exactly that.
All these offers are open to the public, this means that non-members can register as well as members!
Follow us on LinkedIn to never miss an event.

WORKSHOPS

The workshops offered by TUM.ai and our partners provide you with an opportunity to learn about and directly apply software engineering pratices, artificial intelligence and interesting tools.

EVENTS

We organize different events which make it easy to get in touch with other AI enthusiasts.
These events can be Hackathons, Fairs, or Networking events.

TALKS

TUM.ai is in contact with many experts in Artificial Intelligence and related topics, which give talks about their knowledge. Don't miss these opportunities!

Mentoring

TUM.ai collaborates with partner companies and alumni to provide you with mentoring opportunities. That gives you the chance to meet industry professionals who help shape your career. 
This opportunity is only available to our valued members.

WHat we are looking for

Time

To actively engage in the groups operation we expect students to spend around 8 hours a week for TUM.ai. The demand varies depending on which sector you are currently involved in.

initiative

Active engagement of members is what makes the success of this student initiative. Ideally, you have already shown that you have a do-it mind set. 

Student

Since we are a student initiative, it is required that you are enrolled as a student. Which institution does not matter.

AI Knowledge

All TUM.ai members share an interest in Artificial Intelligence. We expect you to have some basic knowledge about the topic, there is no need to be an expert, however!
