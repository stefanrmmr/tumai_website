WELCOME TO TUM.ai


TUM.ai is a student initiative located at the Technical University of Munich.
Together with our highly talented students, we run projects, workshops and events around the topic AI.

OUR MISSION

At TUM.ai, our mission is to connect, execute and learn.

CONNECT

Bring together companies and students through projects and events about AI.

 EXECUTE

Offer students practical experience through projects with peers from the group.

LEARN

Learn more about AI by exchanging with peers and companies, providing workshops and conducting research.

We aim at lowering the entry-bar to AI-creation and -usage for people of every domain by establishing a platform to make practical experience through a diverse set of applied AI projects.
We therefore educate and connect students from diverse backgrounds to incentivize new interdisciplinary AI projects and push the creation and usage of applicable and safe AI in all domains.
