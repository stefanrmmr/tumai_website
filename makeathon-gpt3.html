A 48h challenge for all
innovative thinkers
No programming or AI Skills required

The GPT-3 Makeathon by TUM.ai is a virtual 48-hour challenge where you develop a real-world business case involving AI with a team of talented participants from all backgrounds. Every team gets exclusive access to the most powerful language AI in the world (GPT-3) created by OpenAI, a company co-founded by Elon Musk.

To support you, we will provide helpful workshops and insightful business talks, as well as assistance from mentors and industry experts. Fantastic prizes await the winners, such as a Mentorship Program with TUM Venture Labs to advance your developed idea and support you in founding a start-up.

What is GPT-3?

Language model

Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3 (GPT-3) is a language model that uses deep learning to produce human-like text.

For which tracks could participants develop solutions?

The main idea of our Makeathon is to develop a GPT-3 based solution with a cross-functional team. The solutions you will be working on have to fit into one of four tracks: Legal Tech, Healthcare, Marketing, or Knowledge Management. Within these tracks, you can either join a challenge pitched by one of our industry partners or a participant. If you have an awesome idea yourself make sure to pitch it, so you can convince others to join you in building a viable business concept.

Legal Tech

Join this track if you want to build tools that help law professionals with their work, for example, through solutions in a field of contract law or information aggregation solutions of case-relevant material.

  • Summarize complex legal documents
  • Provide non-binding legal advice to clients
Show examples

Healthcare

This track is all around improving peoples health using the power of AI. This can range from chatbots answering basic health related questions to finding early warning signs of diseases.

  • Generate automatically aggregated medical reports
  • Document important health related incidents of a patient
Show examples

Marketing

In this track, we are looking for a marketing AI application that designs and builds communication tools that can help large corporations improve marketing and provide better customer service.

  • Ideate compelling and unique marketing material
  • Estimate customer sentiments and opinions
Show examples

Knowledge Management

In this track you create AI-based systems which automatically process and provide insights into large amounts of unstructured data, such as summarizing books.

  • Turn interesting books and emails into insightful summaries
  • Help organizations to automatically process and analyze reports
Show examples

Any questions left?

The whole concept of our Makeathon is to build upon the idea of cross-functional teams. Therefore, we are looking forward to all kinds of skill sets relevant for our Makeathon (see question "What if I already have a team?"). AI will impact every domain. Yours too. Join and find out how!

A Makeathon is a fun event that brings together like-minded people from different backgrounds to collaborate and rapidly build new innovative products.

No, you don’t need a team. A team-finding event will take place at the beginning of April where you can form your cross-functional team which can consist of up to 5 people.

In order to fulfil the deliverables "Business Model Canvas" and "first prototype with GPT-3 usage", your team should cover tech, AI, and business skills as well as domain knowledge for your corresponding track (see application formula). As long as this is fulfilled and your team has a max. of 5 people, you can work with whoever you would like. Therefore it will not matter if you already have a team before the start of the event.

Note: Of course, one person can have more than one skill or domain knowledge, for example, two participants could already fulfil the four requirements (tech, AI, and business skill and domain knowledge).

We will provide a jury of experts who will examine your solutions and evaluate them fairly.
