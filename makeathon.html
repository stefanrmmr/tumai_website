A 48h challenge for

No programming or AI Skills required

The GPT-3 Makeathon by TUM.ai is a virtual 48-hour challenge where you develop a real-world business case involving AI with a team of talented participants from all backgrounds. Every team gets exclusive access to the most powerful language AI in the world (GPT-3) created by OpenAI, a company co-founded by Elon Musk.

To support you, we will provide helpful workshops and insightful business talks, as well as assistance from mentors and industry experts. Fantastic prizes await the winners, such as a Mentorship Program with TUM Venture Labs to advance your developed idea and support you in founding a start-up.

What is GPT-3?

Language model

Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3 (GPT-3) is a language model that uses deep learning to produce human-like text.

For which tracks can you develop solutions?

The main idea of our Makeathon is to develop a GPT-3 based solution with a cross-functional team. The solutions you will be working on have to fit into one of four tracks: Legal Tech, Healthcare, Marketing, or Knowledge Management. Within these tracks, you can either join a challenge pitched by one of our industry partners or a participant. If you have an awesome idea yourself make sure to pitch it, so you can convince others to join you in building a viable business concept.

Legal Tech

Join this track if you want to build tools that help law professionals with their work, for example, through solutions in a field of contract law or information aggregation solutions of case-relevant material.

Healthcare

No, you don’t need a team. A team-finding event will take place at the beginning of April where you can form your cross-functional team which can consist of up to 5 people.

In order to fulfil the deliverables "Business Model Canvas" and "first prototype with GPT-3 usage", your team should cover tech, AI, and business skills as well as domain knowledge for your corresponding track (see application formula). Note: Of course, one person can have more than one skill or domain knowledge, for example, two participants could already fulfil the four requirements (tech, AI, and business skill and domain knowledge).

Apply as a Challenge-Setter

As a Makeathon partner, you pitch your idea/challenge to the participants and support with know-how/mentors/material. In exchange, you get a team to work on your idea with the most powerful NLP technology yet!

Being a participating partner means that:

  • You will create a challenge for our Makethon with a clear NLP use case. This will help the participants unleash the full power of GPT-3.
  • You would provide a prize for the top team. Remember prizes attract talents! You can utilize this to get more participants to work on your challenge. The ball is in your court here regarding what you want to offer.
  • You would provide mentors/materials for the duration of the Makeathon. This is of course to offer support to our participants during their Makeathon but also to guarantee an overall higher quality of the solutions. So there is an added benefit to you here as well.

Interested? Then you can submit your ideas for a challenge (preferably a couple of ideas) and we come back to you to further discuss and shape your proposed challenge!

Sounds like a lot of work? Still want to be part of our Makeathon? Then contact us at contact@tum-ai.com and will try make it work.

Here are a couple of guidelines that can help coming up with a challenge:

  • Not too general. The whole point of creating a challenge for the participants is to help them focus on an idea. A too general challenge would prevent that.
  • Not too specific. We want our participants to create new ideas. A too specific idea will moreover prevent the synthesis of more creative ideas.
  • Clear GPT-3 use-case. The problem needs to be solvable with NLP.
